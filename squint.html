the squint test
X

This camara viewed is meant to serve as a tool for designers to evaluate the visibility of their designs. It is often taught in design school to check your layouts by using 'the squint test' and seeing what shapes are most prominent when you blur your vision. This filter allows you to do that, there is a slider for a costumizeable ammount of 'squint'.... test out your best work, will you pass the test?
